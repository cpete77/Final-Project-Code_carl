Germany

In 2022 Moderna sent me to Berlin, Germany for a MES conference. The first three days of the trip I attended the conference and then I stayed 5 more days with my girlfriend as vacation. Berlin was an amazing city with rich history and culture. It was a great time visiting the many museuems, enjoying the diverse cuisine and biking through the city to see landmarks such as the Berlin Wall, Brandenburg Gate and the Reichstag.

Photo Gallery
