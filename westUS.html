Western US

I've taken three trips to see national parks in the western United States. Over the years I saw Glacier, Grand Tetons, Yellowstone, Yosemite, Sequoia, Kings Canyon, Death Valley, Zion, Bryce Canyon, Arches, Canyonlands, Joshua Tree, and the Grand Canyon.
