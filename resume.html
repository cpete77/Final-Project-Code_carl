What is MES

Manufacturing Execution Systems are specialized software solutions used to manage, monitor, and optimize production processes in real time. In the biotech industry, MES plays a crucial role in ensuring the production of high-quality biopharmaceutical products while complying with strict regulatory standards like FDA’s 21 CFR Part 11 and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP).

MES provides real-time tracking of production processes, enabling biotech manufacturers to monitor critical parameters such as temperature, pH, pressure, and other conditions required for biological processes like fermentation or cell culture. MES replaces paper-based batch records with electronic batch records, providing a digital, centralized system to document all production steps and quality checks. This helps reduce errors and streamline batch release processes. MES tracks raw materials, intermediates, and finished products, ensuring proper storage conditions (e.g., cold storage for biological materials) and reducing waste.
